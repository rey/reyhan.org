Palin

10 January 2016

Palin is an app for your menu bar that shows a green dot when you have an internet connection, or a red dot if you don't have an internet connection.

Install

  1. Download the latest version from https://www.strongspace.com/rey/public/Palin.zip
  2. Unzip, drag to your Applications folder
  3. Double-click to launch

Unidentified developer!

You may get a prompt saying something about an an Unidentified developer, if so go to Security & Privacy in your System Preferences and check the Allow apps downloaded from: Anywhere radio button.
